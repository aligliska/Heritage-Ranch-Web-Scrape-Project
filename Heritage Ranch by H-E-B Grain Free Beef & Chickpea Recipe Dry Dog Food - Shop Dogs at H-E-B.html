Feeding for Growth: For puppies ages 4 weeks to 6 months, feed approximately 1/2 to 3/4 cup for every 2 lbs of body weight. For puppies ages 6 months to 12 months, feed approximately 1/3 to 1/2 cup for every 2 lbs of body weight. Feeding for Reproduction: Food consumption may vary during gestation, so feed the amount needed to maintain the pregnant female's good body condition. THEN OVER THE NEXT 7 TO 10 DAYS, GRADUALLY INCREASE THE AMOUNT OF HERITAGE RANCH BY H-E-B® GRAIN FREE ADULT BEEF & CHICKPEA RECIPE DOG FOOD.
